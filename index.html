Does age and location influence internet concerns?

Overall, the data seems to indicate that most online concerns are spread accross all ages and locations. I divided all "Yes" responses (out of n=2245) by county and mapped them according to the age of the respondents. The most frequent concern for was Identity Theft, followed by Credit or Banking Fraud, then Data Collection and Tracking.

How are users accessing the internet?

According to the user responses, most access the internet via laptop or phone, with phone being most users' primary method of accessing the internet. The data indicates that tablets and smart TVs are also growing in popularity.

How are people spending their time on the internet?

Overall, most internet users seem to prioritize communication, shopping, and banking. Communication includes email, texting and social media. Interestingly, over 50% of respondents reported using the internet to access online health records or watch videos online.
